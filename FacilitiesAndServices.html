Our Services

To kick-start your day breakfast is included with the room for all guests serving an “a la carte” menu which includes orange juice, great coffee, tea, chocolate or milk for our young travelers.

Free Wi-Fi internet is available throughout the hotel you will be able to remain in contact with your friends and family back home whilst you enjoy your time away.
