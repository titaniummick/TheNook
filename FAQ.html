Rates & Payment

• Rates are inclusive of prevailing Government tax. Should the percentage of tax vary on room and /or food and beverage, or additional taxes be imposed on these services, such increases or decreases will be passed on to our guest, therefore rates are subject to change without prior notice.

We accepts payment in cash and credit cards (Visa and MasterCard)

Terms and Conditions

• The above rates are quoted and remittance requires in THB net per night inclusive of breakfast, service charge, VAT and non-commissionable.

• Meals Rate: Breakfast is 120 Baht per person / rate is inclusive of 10% service charge and 7% government tax

• Transfer from/to Bangkok domestic or International Airport (one way) by private car start from 1200 - 1500 Baht depend on pick up time

• There is no extra charge for children under 6 years sharing the same room as their parents (limited to a maximum 1 child per room, unless otherwise stated) without extra bed. Additional breakfast will be charged as per contract meal rates.

• Check-in time is 2 p.m.

• Check-out time is 12 p.m. on the day of departure. Late check-out is subject to availability and will be charged at 50% of contracted room rate if check out prior to 6 p.m. and full overnight rate if after 6 p.m.

Cancellations/No show charges

• In case of cancellation written notice must be received by the hotel at least 7 days prior to the arrival date. In the event that this written notice is not received as stipulated, and in the event that there is a no-show the following charges are applicable without exception:

Low Season - 1 night charge

High and Peak Season - 2 nights charge

Smoking Policy

THE NooK is strictly a smoke free environment, smoking is allowed outside only

Commitment to the community and the environment THE NooK has a mission that is to reduce, reuse and recycle we have implemented a voluntary linen and towel reuse in all rooms.
